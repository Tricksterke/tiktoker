Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I make a payment?
You can make a payment using various methods, including M-Pesa, PayPal, Skrill, Remitly, Mukuru, and various cryptocurrencies. Simply choose your preferred method during the checkout process.
2. What payment methods do you accept?
We accept payments through M-Pesa, PayPal, Skrill, Remitly, Mukuru, and various cryptocurrencies.
3. How can I track my order?
Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this tracking number to monitor the status and location of your shipment.
4. What is your return policy?
Our return policy allows you to return items within 30 days of purchase. Please visit our 'Returns and Exchanges' page for detailed information on the return process and eligibility.
5. Do you offer international shipping?
Yes, we offer international shipping to most countries. Shipping costs and delivery times may vary based on your location. Please check our 'Shipping Information' page for details.
6. Can I modify or cancel my order?
Once an order is placed, modifications or cancellations may not be possible. Please contact our customer support as soon as possible if you need assistance with your order.
