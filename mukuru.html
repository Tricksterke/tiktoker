Mukuru Payment Guide

Follow these steps to make a payment with Mukuru:

  1. GO TO YOUR MUKURU account OR USE MUKURU USSD - *130*566#
  2. SELECT SEND MONEY
  3. Enter COUNTRY - KENYA
  4. CHOOSE - WALLET - MPESA
  5. ENTER MPESA NUMBER +254768290466
  6. ENTER AMOUNT
  7. SEND
