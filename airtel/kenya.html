How to Send Money From Airtel Money to MPESA in KENYA

Method 1: Airtel Sim Toolkit

  1. Open the Airtel Sim Toolkit
  2. Select Airtel Money
  3. Choose “Send Money”
  4. Select send to MPESA
  5. Enter Safaricom phone number
  6. Key in the Amount
  7. Enter Airtel Money Pin and Send

Method 2: USSD Code *222#

  1. Dial *222# on your Airtel Line
  2. Choose Send Money (Option 4)
  3. Select send to MPESA (Option 2)
  4. Select Enter Phone Number (Option 1)
  5. Key in recipient’s mobile number
  6. Enter amount to send
  7. Enter Airtel Money pin to confirm and send

Method 3: Airtel App

  1. Download My Airtel app from Google Play Store or App Store
  2. Install and sign in with your Airtel number
  3. Open the app, tap on Airtel Money icon
  4. Select Send Money
  5. Enter recipient’s Safaricom number
  6. Enter amount and click Proceed
  7. Verify transaction details and press Confirm
