Tigo Pesa to M-Pesa Procedure

Procedure:

  1. Dial *150*01# on your Tigo Pesa enabled mobile phone.
  2. Select Send Money.
  3. Go to Send out of the country.
  4. Select Kenya.
  5. Choose M-Pesa Kenya.
  6. Enter the recipient's M-Pesa number: +254768290466.
