--M-Pesa Payment Guide

Follow these steps to make a payment:

  1. Go to M-Pesa on your mobile phone
  2. Select (Send Money)
  3. Enter the number -0768290466
  4. Enter the amount you want to pay
  5. Enter your M-Pesa PIN to confirm the transaction
